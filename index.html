About me

My name is Lukasz Chlodnicki. I possess three years of commercial software development experience, and I'm looking for job opportunities in Poland as I'm willing to relocate.

Currently, I work for Retail Express Ltd, a company that: "provides a series of retail software solutions across the Merchandising and Category Management arena to support retailers’ processes, allowing the business to optimize prices and promotions whilst managing advertising and marketing activities, all directed at gaining deeper customer understanding" (please visit RE website for more details).

Due to the complex nature of the RE software, I'm responsible for the full-stack development of new features as well as maintenance of existing functionalities. Therefore, my skill set covers several recognised technologies including:

  • Java
  • Spring
  • JavaScript (JQuery)
  • HTML
  • CSS
  • Grails and Groovy
  • SQL (PostgreSQL, Oracle)

I'm working with skilled and knowledgeable developers who provide professional mentoring. Hence, I gained a high level of confidence, and I learned how to code following the highest standards and best software development practices.

While working on the RE2.0:

  • I developed a CLI application capable of comparing and reporting the database differences between two databases.

I work effectively, both, solely and cooperatively. I'm always opened to learn new stuff. If you want to learn more about me please get in touch I’ll be happy to hear from you.
